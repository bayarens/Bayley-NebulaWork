The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense plantings, and a mixture of ornamelntal and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-invented in 1870's England, when stylized versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained estate gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern desendants, with an emphasis on vegtables and herbs, aling with some fruit trees.
